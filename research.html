Research at the University of Oxford

The University of Oxford is renowned for its groundbreaking research across various disciplines. Our research activities span from fundamental scientific discoveries to applied research with significant societal impacts.

Key Research Areas

  • Humanities: Explore the history, literature, philosophy, and languages that shape our understanding of cultures and societies.
  • Sciences: From physics to biology, chemistry to computer science, our scientists push the boundaries of knowledge and innovation.
  • Social Sciences: Understand human behavior, economics, politics, and law through rigorous research and analysis.
  • Medical Sciences: Improve health and well-being through medical research, clinical trials, and healthcare innovations.

Research Institutes and Centers

Oxford hosts several world-leading research institutes and centers that foster collaborative research and interdisciplinary studies:

  • Oxford Internet Institute (OII)
  • Oxford Martin School
  • Wellcome Centre for Ethics and Humanities
  • Smith School of Enterprise and the Environment

Our researchers collaborate globally, pushing the boundaries of knowledge and addressing some of the world's most pressing challenges.
